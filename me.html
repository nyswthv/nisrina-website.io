All about Me ♡


Hi, I'm Nur Nisrina Binti Norazali, born and raised in Seremban, Negeri Sembilan. I was born on 15 April 2005, which means I recently turned 20,believe me, I still can’t quite wrap my head around the fact that my teenage years are officially over! It feels surreal to think that I’m now stepping into adulthood, with all the responsibilities and expectations that come with it. Learning to carry myself more like an adult is still a work in progress, but I'm embracing it one step at a time. I’m currently pursuing my Diploma in Library Informatics at UiTM Merbok, Kedah, now in my fourth semester,just one more to go before I complete this chapter of my academic journey. I’ve always had a curious mind and a heart full of diverse passions. From knowledge systems to the mysteries of the world around us, I find inspiration in both the seen and the unseen. While I’m still discovering where my path may lead, I believe that having many interests is a strength. It allows me to see the world through multiple lenses, to stay open, creative, and constantly learning. This space is where I share a bit of who I am and where I'm headed. Whether you're here out of curiosity or connection, I’m truly glad you stopped by.
